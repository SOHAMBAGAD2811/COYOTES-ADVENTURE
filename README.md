## вңЁ FEATURES

### рҹ–ҘпёҸ Full Diegetic Terminal UI
Everything you see IS the game. No menus, no HUD overlays вҖ” the entire interface is the Coyote's rover terminal, complete with:
- **CRT scanlines, film grain, and screen flicker** вҖ” authentic retro-terminal feel
- **Ambient dust particles** floating across the viewport
- **Camera shake** on failed breach attempts
- **Terminal flash** effects for success/failure feedback

### рҹҺҜ Custom Target Cursor
A spinning bracket cursor that locks onto interactive elements, switches to a glowing hand during gameplay, and snaps to button boundaries on hover.

### рҹ”Ҡ Dynamic Soundtrack
Adaptive audio engine that crossfades between tracks based on the current mission stage вҖ” tense during hacking, calm during intercom transmissions.

### рҹ“– Cinematic Story Sequence
Glitch-animated story cards with speech synthesis narration, starfield backgrounds, and a full boot sequence with typewriter diagnostics.

### рҹҢЎпёҸ Real-Time Systems
- **Heat Telemetry** вҖ” core temperature rises during the water stage; vent coolant or face system failure
- **Mission Clock** вҖ” 6-minute countdown with convoy progress bar
- **Battery drain** and **coordinate drift** for immersion
- **Mission Log** вҖ” real-time scrolling log of every action, warning, and success
